Renewable Energy Sources

Solar Energy

Harness the power of the sun with photovoltaic panels or solar thermal systems. Ideal for areas with high sun exposure and suitable for both residential and commercial applications.

Efficiency: 15-22% | Lifespan: 25-30 years

Wind Energy

Convert wind power into electricity with turbines of various sizes. Most effective in open areas with consistent wind patterns, from small residential turbines to larger installations.

Efficiency: 30-45% | Lifespan: 20-25 years

Geothermal Energy

Utilize the stable temperature of the earth for heating and cooling. Geothermal heat pumps can be installed in most locations and provide highly efficient climate control.

Efficiency: 300-500% | Lifespan: 25-50 years

Energy Storage

Maximize your renewable energy system with battery storage solutions. Store excess energy for use during peak demand periods or when your renewable sources aren't generating.

Efficiency: 80-95% | Lifespan: 10-15 years

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the recommendations? +
Our recommendations are based on detailed climate data, local energy costs, and efficiency models of modern renewable energy systems. In most cases, our energy production estimates are within 10-15% of actual performance. Many factors can influence actual results, including weather variations, equipment quality, and installation precision.
What types of renewable energy do you recommend? +
We analyze and recommend various renewable energy sources including solar (photovoltaic and thermal), wind, geothermal, micro-hydro, and biomass systems. We also provide recommendations on energy storage solutions such as batteries and thermal storage, as well as smart energy management systems to optimize your energy usage.
Do you account for local incentives and rebates? +
Yes! Our system maintains a comprehensive database of federal, state, and local incentives, tax credits, rebates, and renewable energy certificate programs. When you enter your location, we automatically factor these into our financial calculations to give you the most accurate picture of your investment and returns.
Can I integrate with my existing energy systems? +
Absolutely. Our recommendations take into account your existing infrastructure. Whether you have a conventional HVAC system, an older solar installation, or want to integrate with your smart home, we'll provide guidance on the best integration approach to maximize efficiency and minimize costs.
How long does it take to recoup my investment? +
The payback period varies based on your location, energy consumption, chosen system, and available incentives. For most residential solar installations, payback periods range from 5-10 years. Wind and geothermal systems typically have payback periods of 7-15 years. Our calculator provides a detailed payback analysis specific to your situation.
